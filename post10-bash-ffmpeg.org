* To convert from *wav* to *mp3* run:
  
    #+BEGIN_SRC bash
    ffmpeg -i 01\ -\ My\ Song.wav -acodec mp3 -ab 360K 01\ -\ My\ Song.mp3
    #+END_SRC

* To convert from *m4a* to *mp3* run:

    #+BEGIN_SRC bash
    ffmpeg -v 5 -y -i 01\ -\ My\ Song.m4a -ac 2 -ab 360k 02\ -\ My\ Song.mp3
    #+END_SRC
